Why Did Exness Pause Indian Onboarding?

The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has intensified scrutiny of unregulated online forex and CFD trading, particularly after several offshore brokers, including OctaFX and FBS, ran afoul of local laws. Sanctions and high-profile raids have made foreign brokers wary, prompting Exness to halt new Indian registrations.

Key Reasons for the Exness India Pause

  • Crackdown on offshore CFD/forex trading and prop firms by RBI
  • Recent raids, asset freezes, and negative publicity targeting big brands
  • Stricter enforcement around reverse solicitation (i.e., users seeking brokers, not vice versa)
  • Indian regulatory reforms: higher Securities Transaction Tax (STT), new limits on leverage, and retail trading restrictions
