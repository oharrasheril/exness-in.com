Forex, CFDs & Indian Law

Legal Forex in India:

  • Only INR pairs (USD/INR, EUR/INR, JPY/INR, GBP/INR)
  • Only on NSE/BSE or SEBI-registered brokers

Illegal/Gray Market:

  • All non-INR pairs (e.g. EUR/USD, GBP/USD)
  • Gold, oil, global indices, crypto CFDs
  • Any trading via offshore websites like Exness, OctaFX, XM, FBS
