The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) have always maintained a prohibition on retail trading of forex and CFDs in pairs not involving the Indian Rupee. This policy, designed to prevent capital flight and ensure stability, intensified in 2025‚Äì2026 after high-profile crackdowns against offshore brokers such as OctaFX and FBS.
